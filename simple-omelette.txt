Ingredients:
  -4 eggs
  -2 cups cheddar cheese shredded
  -1/4 lbs thin sliced turkey
  -1/4 cup diced onions
  -1/2 oz butter

  Directions:
  1. beat eggs in a large bowl until smooth consistency
  2. tear turkey into small pieces and store on a small dish
  3. in a small frying pan, sautee onions with butter until golden brown
  4. add turkey to onions and stir together
  5. in large non-stick frying pan, on med heat, fry eggs until solid enough to flip.
  6. flip egg over
  7. add blanket of cheese, turkey and onions
  8. fold over and remove from heat
  9. allow to cool a little and enjoy!
